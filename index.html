I studied Translation (German / English / Bosnian / Croatian / Serbian) and Digital Humanities. Since 2012 I have worked as a research assistant at the Centre for Information Modelling (ZIM) at the University of Graz. My PhD project is about Linked Data and the potential of using Wikidata as a hub between the Life Sciences and the Humanities. My main topics of interest are Semantic Web technologies, Linked Data, and Web Programming. I am teaching university classes in all of these fields.

Bio & Events

Bio

Holds a Master’s degree in Translation (German/English/Bosnian/Croatian/Serbian) and a Joint Master’s Degree in Digital Heritage and Digital Humanities (EuroMACHS). Currently he is a PhD candidate in Digital Humanities. Since 2012 he has been a research assistant at the Centre for Information Modelling (ZIM) at the University of Graz. His work includes technical development, data modelling and data wrangling in various DH projects. The main focus of his work is on Semantic Web technologies, Linked Data, and Web Programming. In addition to teaching in these fields, his dissertation project is about Linked Data and the potential of using Wikidata as a hub between the Life Sciences and the Humanities.
